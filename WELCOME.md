# Welcome to OpenClaw 🐾

Hey — you made it. You now have your own AI assistant, running right here on your machine.

No cloud accounts. No subscriptions to manage. Just you and your assistant, ready to go.

## What is this?

OpenClaw is a personal AI assistant that lives on your hardware. It can chat with you, remember things, help you think, and connect to you on WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, or just right here in the browser.

It gets smarter the more you use it.

## Try these right now

1. **Ask it anything** — "What's a good recipe for lazy Sunday pasta?"
2. **Check the weather** — "What's the weather like in Tokyo?"
3. **Have it remember something** — "Remember that my anniversary is March 12th"
4. **Give it a personality** — "Let's set up who you are" (it'll walk you through customizing your assistant's vibe)

## Make it yours

Your assistant has a file called `SOUL.md` in its workspace — that's its personality. Edit it to change how it talks, what it cares about, and what kind of assistant it is. Formal? Casual? Snarky? Your call.
